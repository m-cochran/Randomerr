About

Born and raised in Pittsburgh, PA, 2kool is a hip-hop artist with a vision as bold as the city skyline. From the city that forged me to sessions that never cool down, he transforms life, struggle, and ambition into bars and beats. Known for his gritty flow, authentic lyrics, and relentless work ethic, 2kool is more than an artist — he’s a storyteller, a motivator, and a rising voice for his city. Every track is a reflection of his journey: from Pittsburgh blocks to global stages, 2kool is on a mission to turn dreams into music and music into a movement. With a sound rooted in real experiences and a drive that never quits, he’s building not just songs, but a legacy.
